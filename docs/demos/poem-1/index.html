rain

spreads water to hair

coalesces

to a translucent sheet

a cone

of dewy plastic


enclosed, splashy

green stems

they wind their way

a step backwards


and backwards

followed closely by

another

they wind their way

home

everything you already know

dawn comes whispering

comes down from

the trees


you walk so lightly

you carry the wind

under your feet

a certain weight

of tapered wood

of curling arc


everything you can throw

to the wind

floats on back

to you

instead of being a snow-eater

winds rise in the north

when free

of confining fjords


acquire heat and moisture

frictional contrasts

that disconnect the flow

oscillations

the dust in your brain

settles like swept dunes

under a blanket of silence


a ghost paces

in neat and rhythmic lines

tracing stillness


a window

you open

and it is all around you

albedo of glaciers

In summer it is most persistent

persistent

blanket of thick moisture-rich clouds

Warmed by descent

from the crest

forcing lake waves into the mouths of rivers


A clatter of saucer

and cup appears

the receding tide of foam

to soft brown lace

yawning for air

that rustles

colder air is

denser

and it sinks.

holding diaphragm, rising breath


the sound, that rustles

every thing

is quieter in the shadow

of a leaf

it is as if

to the wind

three sheets
