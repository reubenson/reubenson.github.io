Selected Ceramic Works

My work with clay began in 2019, though it was a medium I had been thinking about for a long time beforehand. I think of my ceramics pratice as an extension of my work with sound, in which I had increasingly focused on aspects of form, surface, and color. Looking back, Weaving Music project is perhaps the clearest bridge from my sound-based work to ceramics.
