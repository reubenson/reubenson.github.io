Allure of Inertia

Moving into the spring of ‘24, I made this mix in response to NTS’ call for submissions on the theme of metamorphosis. This mix includes selections that had felt resonant to me over my sabbatical year in 2023. Although there are some recurring motifs (organ music, post-minimal music, mythopoetic free jazz), this mix outlines an energy that I associate with not so much metamorphosis itself, but the amphibious and pulsing capacity for change, a necessary precursor for metamorphosis, for all life on earth.
