A brief ceramics update: I’ll be selling some wares as part of a pop-up sale curated and hosted by Pyperbleu in Williamsburg. Open Sat-Sun August 23-24 from 12-6pm, with a reception at 3pm Saturday.

“Thus the miniscule, a narrow gate, opens up an entire world. The details of a thing can be the sign of a new world,” writes Bachelard in The Poetics of Space. Taken somewhat literally, the travel vases I’ve been making over the years have led me to some bigger pieces this year. Earlier this summer I exhibited a new sculptural work, After Orpheus, for a show at Millenium Film Workshop curated by Michèl Saint-Michel. And in the Pyperbleu show, I’ll be debuting the first of a series of lamps.
