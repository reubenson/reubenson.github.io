A Listening Event

ḟԻ✺❡ ḉℏ✺Ի𓆏ṳṧ

Walk in a slow, undirected manner with your phone (frog) until you find a good place to set it down. Notice how the other participants are setting down their phones (frogs) too, and remain quiet as the frogs (phones) settle into their environment, slowly beginning to make their calls. Focus on the sound of your frog, apart from the other frogs. Wander further away from your frog, until you can no longer distinguish which which frog is yours. Consider what it feels like to be separated from your frog (phone), witnessing it absorbed in the act of listening and singing? After a while, leave the frog chorus, either bringing your phone with you, or not.
