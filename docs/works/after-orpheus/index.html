After Orpheus

Year 2025 Media mixed media Dimensions 32" x 32" x 80"

After Orpheus is a mixed media sculpture that combines ceramics with chrome armature, steel piano wire and electronics to produce acoustically amplified sound without speakers. A molded paper cup held against the self-oscillating wire allows for an intimate mode of listening while also affecting the vibrational modes of the wire.
