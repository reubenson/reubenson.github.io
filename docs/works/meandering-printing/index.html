Meander

Year 2023 Media ceramics Dimensions 8" x 7" x 4"

Meander uses the single, continuous line of a ceramic 3D-printer to translate the Greek key ornamentation into the form of a porcelain vessel. It offers an invitation to the eye to meander with the vessel while blurring the line between interior and exterior space.
